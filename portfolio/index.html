About

Laura is a senior at the University of Maryland pursuing a degree in Criminology/Criminal Justice and Broadcast Journalism. You might see her columns in the Montgomery Sentinel where she discusses all things sports and local news. You can catch her doing what she loves around campus reporting for Terrapin Sports Central or producing live broadcasts for the Big Ten Network at the Xfinity Center! When she is not reporting or writing, she enjoys spending time with her twin Amanda, reading books, and traveling.
